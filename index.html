MY JOURNEY

My musical journey began back when I was in 8th grade, and it’s been an exciting path ever since. Currently, I’m in college, but I took a unique route by doing B-tech together to pursue my passion for music. Inspired by legendary singers like Arijit Singh, Mohit Chauhan, KK, Sonu Nigam, and Shreya Ghoshal, I strive to create music that connects with people on a deep level. Whether it's through instrumental melodies or heartfelt vocals, music has always been my way of expressing myself and reaching out to others.
